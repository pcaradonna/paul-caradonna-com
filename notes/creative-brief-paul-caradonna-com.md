# Creative Brief: paul-caradonna.com

## Purpose
Paul CaraDonna's academic research website. Classically academic in feel — straightforward, clear, professional. Counterpart to total-ecology.net (which is more exploratory/creative).

## Audience
Academic colleagues, students, prospective collaborators, funding agencies, press.

## Tone & Feel
- Clean, minimal, authoritative
- Warm through photography — not cold or sterile
- Monospace type gives it a quiet, consistent identity shared with total-ecology.net
- Film photography (RMBL landscapes) grounds it in place and fieldwork

## References
- https://archive.pointnever.com/ — logged for possible reference (unsure which site this will inform)

## Design Direction
- Black background, white Menlo type (consistent with total-ecology.net)
- Homepage: title text at top, large full-width landscape photo below
- Photo rotates randomly on each page load (6 film photos in assets/)
- No animation — still, considered, calm
